(NEW) Ligands are extracted from your coordinates and searched against the released Chemical Component Dictionary (CCD) for an exact match.

If no match is found in the CCD you will be asked to provide more information about your ligand. No match might be because your ligand is new to the CCD, your ligand isn't released or the coordinates of your ligand are incomplete.

If there are any differences between the coordinates of your ligand and the top match from the CCD, you will be asked to provide more information about your ligand. These differences can be because the coordinates of your ligand are incomplete or the geometry of your ligand does not exactly match the CCD.

Click on "Inspect Selected Ligands" to examine in more detail those ligands which require your attention.

When you have addressed all the issues, pressing "Back to Summary View" will allow you to review the high-level inventory of your ligands to verify that all mismatches are addressed, and pressing "Finish (all issues are addressed)" will allow you to complete the ligand validation.

If any ligand is the focus of the research, please check the corresponding checkbox in the last column below, or indicate that none of them are.
